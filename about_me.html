Last year I went to a surf competition in a small town in central Portugal called Peniche. The weather the first few days was extremely windy, and the ocean looked like a crazy cauldron. By day 3, the weather turned and the ocean had crisp blue lines stretching from the jetty to the sandbank in front of the event site.
